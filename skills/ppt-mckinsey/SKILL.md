## 📊 Design & Copy Heuristics

### 1. MECE Slide Messaging
- Ensure all slides follow the **MECE** (Mutually Exclusive, Collectively Exhaustive) principle.
- **Action Titles**: Slide headers must state a clear, action-oriented takeaway, not just a topic.
  - *Incorrect*: "Revenue Metrics Q4"
  - *Correct*: "Q4 Revenue grew 18% driven by Enterprise Tier adoption"

### 2. High-Density Consulting Layouts
- **Rule of Thirds**: Decompose layouts into 3 vertical columns for clear, scan-optimized information parsing.
- **Horizontal Flow**: Lead with a summary card on the left, followed by cascading details to the right.
- **Visual Hierarchies**: Leverage OKLCH-curated accent colors and bold numbers to call out primary statistics.

### 3. Chart.js & PPTX Integrations
- Programmatically generate responsive slides via HTML slide decks.
- Integrate Chart.js with precise data inputs for responsive pitch decks.
